quick guide

There are two services you can use

  • Get spotify n amount of playlists based on a search word
  • Get n amount of top tracks based on any given country

Spotify playlists: "/info/playlist/WORD/SIZE"

Top tracks from a given country: "/info/top/COUNTRY ABBREVIATION/SIZE"

The data will be contained by the tag "p" that has a class of "data"
